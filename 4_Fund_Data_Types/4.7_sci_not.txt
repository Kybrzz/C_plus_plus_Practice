==== 4.7: Intro to Scientific Notation ====

1. Key Takeaways
- scientific notation = shorthand for inputting lengthy numbers in a succinct manner
- form of scientific notation = significand * 10^exponent 
- forms: 
	* 1.2 * 10^4 = 1.2e4
	** 5 * 10^-2 = 5e-2 
- significant digits = digits in the significand
